WELCOME TO MY PAGE

Hi! I am Michael, currently a Data Science Master candidate at Duke University. I was born in Chengdu, China, but I move to Toronto, Canada at 14. I have a Bachelor's degree and a Master's degree in Biomedical Engineering, and I have previously spent over a year working in the healthcare space. It was at that time I learned the power of big data and a career in Data Science. Therefore, I was determined to become a Data Scientist. You can learn more about this transition in my Medium blog post!

I am passionate about exploring the interaction between data and technology, and utilizing data-driven techniques to uncover and understand patterns, and ultimately translating them into organic insights that could enhance decision-making processes! Furthermore, I am interested in experiences that are collaborative, fast-paced, and cultivating. Because of my background in healthcare, I am excited about scalable data solutions that are also socially meaningful.

On the personal end, I love bouldering and working out, I find them very effective at relieving my stress and improving my focus. I also enjoy cooking and I am a big foodie. Having lived in a diverse city like Toronto, I have the fortune to try some fo the most authentic exotic cuisines.
